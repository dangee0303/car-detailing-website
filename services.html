What We Specialize In

At PrimeShine Auto Detailing, we focus on precision, protection, and results. Every service is designed to maintain and enhance your vehicle’s appearance while ensuring long-term care.

Exterior Detailing

Hand wash, polish, and wax. We remove dirt and imperfections while restoring the paint’s shine and protecting it from the elements.

Interior Detailing

Deep cleaning of carpets, seats, and all surfaces. Stain removal, vacuuming, and sanitization leave your cabin fresh and hygienic.

Ceramic Coating

Long-lasting protective layer for your car’s paint. Guards against UV, dirt, scratches, and enhances gloss for years to come.
